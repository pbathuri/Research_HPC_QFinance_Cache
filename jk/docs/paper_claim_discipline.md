# Paper Claim Discipline

All major claims must include:

1. source artifact dependency
2. evidence level
3. strengthening requirement (if not measured)

Allowed evidence labels:

- `measured`
- `derived`
- `proxy-supported`
- `hypothesis`
- `deferred`

Disallowed writing pattern:

- asserting hardware cache improvement from Mac-side proxies alone

Required disclaimer:

- similarity/guided-cache outputs are architecture hypotheses unless validated by
  controlled experiments and PMU-backed HPC studies.
