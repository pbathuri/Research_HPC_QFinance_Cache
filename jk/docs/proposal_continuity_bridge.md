# Proposal Continuity Bridge

This bridge explains why the current empirical path is a methodological
strengthening of the original proposal, not a detour.

## Proposal continuity

- Constant themes preserved:
  - finance workloads
  - cache/reuse structure
  - HPC and eventual QHPC relevance

## Re-scoping rationale

- The project shifted to evidence-first staged development:
  - workload family construction
  - unified observability
  - similarity and guided-cache hypotheses
  - paper-ready packaging

This re-scope reduced over-claim risk and improved reproducibility.

## Why this is stronger

  reproducible artifacts for each stage.
